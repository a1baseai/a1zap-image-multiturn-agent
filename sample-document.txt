ACME Corporation Employee Handbook
===================================

Company Overview
----------------
ACME Corporation is a leading provider of innovative solutions since 1985. 
We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Work Hours
----------
Standard office hours are Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
Remote work is available on Wednesdays and Fridays with manager approval.

Vacation Policy
---------------
- New employees: 10 days per year
- 2-5 years: 15 days per year
- 5+ years: 20 days per year

Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal.

Sick Leave
----------
Employees receive 5 sick days per year. Additional sick leave may be granted
with a doctor's note. Sick leave does not roll over to the next year.

Benefits
--------
- Health Insurance: Full coverage starts after 90 days
- 401(k): 5% company match
- Gym Membership: Reimbursement up to $50/month
- Professional Development: Up to $2,000 per year for courses and certifications

Dress Code
----------
Business casual attire is expected in the office. Jeans are permitted on Fridays.
When meeting with clients, business professional attire is required.

Company Values
--------------
1. Innovation: We constantly seek new and better ways to serve our customers
2. Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency
3. Teamwork: We collaborate and support each other
4. Excellence: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do

Emergency Procedures
--------------------
In case of fire:
1. Evacuate immediately using the nearest exit
2. Do not use elevators
3. Gather at the designated meeting point in the parking lot
4. Wait for the all-clear from building management

For medical emergencies, call 911 and notify building security.
